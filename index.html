Reinhard Lüftenegger

Cryptography Researcher and Engineer
Currently working as cryptography engineer with Shielded Technologies on Midnight.

Graduation with Highest Honors sub auspiciis praesidentis rei publicae as PhD from Graz University of Technology in the Cryp­to­lo­gy & Privacy group at ISEC. Research in cryptography on the design and analysis of cryptographic primitives tailored for modern (zero-knowledge) proof systems.

Broad research interests include applied cryptography, (zero-knowledge) SNARKS, and digital privacy. Passionate about the social value and mathematics of cryptography.

With a weakness for riding bicycles over remote mountains.
